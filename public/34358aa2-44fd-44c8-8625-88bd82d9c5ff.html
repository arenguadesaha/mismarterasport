Diego Leon's Manchester United squad number revealed amid pre-season hint

Manchester United have confirmed summer signing Diego Leon will wear the No.30 shirt in his debut season at Old Trafford.

The left-back finalised his switch to United earlier this month, arriving from Paraguayan club Cerro Porteno. It is understood he has signed a long-term contract.

After being unveiled as a United player on July 5, he started training alongside other members of United's first-team squad on the opening day of pre-season training on July 7. It is yet to be determined how big a role he might have in Ruben Amorim's plans this coming season.

The 18-year-old has been present in first-team training so far this summer and is poised to get the chance to try and impress Amorim over the coming weeks.

United will play five pre-season friendlies ahead of their Premier League opener on August 17 against Arsenal.

The Reds have have said United supporters could catch a first glimpse of Leon when they face Leeds United at the Friends Arena in Stockholm, Sweden, on Saturday.

For the third season running, United are starting their pre-season campaign in Scandinavia.

Leon is one of two United imports who could be given their unofficial debuts against Leeds, with Matheus Cunha also in contention to secure his first outing for the club.
