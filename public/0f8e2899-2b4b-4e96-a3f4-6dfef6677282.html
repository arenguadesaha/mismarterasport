Arsenal pre-season fixtures in full as Mikel Arteta plans laid bare

The new 2025/26 Premier League season is now less than a month from kicking off and that means pre-season is under way.

Arsenal are already back in training and have spent the last few days in Spain for a warm weather training camp.

Soon they will be heading off to Asia for their usual pre-season tour, as they make the trip to Singapore and then Hong Kong for three matches away.

Manager Mikel Arteta will be guaranteed to have three new faces with him on the tour, with new signings Kepa Arrizabalaga, Martin Zubimendi and Christian Norgaard already joining up with their new teammates.

Arteta will be hoping to have one or two more with him too, with Cristhian Mosquera likely to have signed on before the team travel out on Saturday, July 19. There are question marks over Viktor Gyokeres though, with Arteta keen to have his new striker in place ahead of the tour.

Arsenal and Sporting CP remain in talks over the breakdown of the fee that has already been agreed between the two clubs for the Swede to sign for the Gunners. Noni Madueke will likely join up with his new Arsenal teammates at some point during the tour, having been given extra time off due to his participation with Chelsea at the Club World Cup.

After the tour is over, the Gunners will head home for two more pre-season fixtures in the Emirates Cup at their home stadium.
