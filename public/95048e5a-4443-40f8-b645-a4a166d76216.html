Manchester United reveal new away shirt for 2025/2026 Premier League season

Manchester United have revealed their new away shirt for the 2025/2026 season.

The kit is predominantly white and light lavender with dark purple accents.

The design has been inspired by the 1990-92 away kit and has a snowflake pattern. It includes a shirt with an overlapping v-collar and devil logo and will be worn with purple shorts.

Last month, United launched their new home kit for the 2025/2026 campaign and the first sightings of the home and away kits will come during this summer's pre-season friendly schedule.

The players reported back to Carrington for the beginning of pre-season on July 7 and will play their opening friendly of the summer against Leeds in Stockholm, Sweden on July 19.

United will subsequently travel to the United States to face West Ham, Bournemouth and Everton in the Premier League Summer Series.

The Reds will play their final pre-season friendly against Fiorentina at Old Trafford on August 9.

United have made just one signing, Matheus Cunha, in the summer transfer window so far, and negotiations with Brentford over a move for forward Bryan Mbeumo have stalled.

Brentford have rejected two United bids for Mbeumo and a compromise is yet to be reached.
