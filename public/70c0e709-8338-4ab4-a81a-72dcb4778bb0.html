Manchester United confirm 29-man travelling squad for pre-season friendly fixture vs Leeds

Manchester United head coach Ruben Amorim has included new signings Diego Leon and Matheus Cunha in his 29-man travelling squad for Saturday's pre-season friendly against Leeds United in Sweden.

United will kick off their pre-season preparations with a warm up match against their Premier League counterparts at the Strawberry Arena in Stockholm.

It will mark the first of five friendlies United will fulfil this summer, with Amorim and his players jetting off to the United States next Tuesday ahead of playing against West Ham United, Bournemouth and Everton in the Premier League Summer Series.

They will then face Fiorentina at Old Trafford on August 9.

The trip to Scandinavia will give United's supporters a chance to catch a first glimpse of Leon and Cunha, who arrived at Old Trafford earlier this summer. Cunha arrived from Wolves last month for Â£62.5million, while Leon sealed a switch from Paraguayan club Cerro Porteno a fortnight ago.

The 29-man squad selected by Amorim features a blend of both youth and experience. There is a chance the United boss could field two entirely different XIs in either half against Daniel Farke's side.

The youngsters to have made the cut include Tyler Fredricson, Reece Munro, Godwill Kukonki, Sekou Kone, Jack Fletcher and Bendito Mantato.

However, Harry Amass has been omitted from Amorim's plans amid the expectation that he is going to be sent out on loan.

Elsewhere, United, as expected, will be without Andre Onana and Lisandro Martinez. The latter is still recovering from an anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) injury and Onana is sidelined after suffering a hamstring injury in training last week.

The likes of Matthijs de Ligt, Leny Yoro, Harry Maguire, Mason Mount, Bruno Fernandes, Rasmus Hojlund and Amad have all made the cut.

Goalkeepers: Altay Bayindir, Tom Heaton & Dermot Mee.

Defenders: Diogo Dalot, Noussair Mazraoui, Matthijs de Ligt, Harry Maguire, Leny Yoro, Ayden Heaven, Tyler Fredricson, Reece Munro, Godwill Kukonki, Luke Shaw, Patrick Dorgu & Diego Leon.

Midfielders: Casemiro, Manuel Ugarte, Kobbie Mainoo, Toby Collyer, Mason Mount, Bruno Fernandes, Jack Fletcher & Sekou Kone.

Forwards: Amad, Matheus Cunha, Rasmus Hojlund, Chido Obi, Ethan Williams & Bendito Mantato.
